### Keyboard Shortcuts
**What:** Add keyboard shortcuts for power users — arrow keys for slide nav, Cmd+Enter for export, number keys to jump to slides, Escape to focus textarea.
**Why:** The Growth Lead making 2+ carousels/week will discover and use shortcuts quickly. Professional feel, faster workflow for repeat users.
**Context:** The 3-panel layout has natural focus zones (textarea, preview, settings). Shortcuts should respect which zone has focus — arrow keys in textarea = normal text editing, arrow keys when preview focused = slide navigation.
**Effort:** S (human: ~2 hours / CC: ~10 min)
**Depends on:** Phase 5 (Assembly) complete
